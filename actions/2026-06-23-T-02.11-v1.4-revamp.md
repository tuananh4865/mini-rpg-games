# T-02.11: v1.4 — Realistic physics + 2.5D + infinite city

## User request
4 requests:
1. Đồ hoạ 2.5D nổi khối, chi tiết hơn
2. Điều khiển xe khó - xe đi ngang thay vì theo đầu/đuôi
3. Cho phép cướp vào bất cứ xe nào trong thành phố
4. Thành phố mở rộng vô hạn

## Implementation

### 1. Realistic Ackermann vehicle physics
- v.angle → v.heading (heading = direction FRONT of car points)
- Bicycle model: turnRate = (fSteer * speed) / wheelbase
- Per-vehicle wheelbase (38/36/48/22 for sedan/sports/truck/motor)
- Smooth steering lerp (8x per second)
- MAX_STEER = 0.6 rad (~34°)
- Reverse = flip turn direction
- Brake light visualization

### 2. 2.5D graphics
- Buildings: south + east side walls visible (12px tall)
- Strong ground shadows (sun from top-left)
- Roof highlight (NW) + shadow (SE)
- 3x3 window grid with cross frames + lit glow halos
- Door with knob
- Vehicles: 2.5D body (side panels, top edge highlight, dark bottom)
- Headlights/taillights clearly distinguished
- Steering indicator on player car

### 3. Steal any car
- Enter (E) checks BOTH vehicles[] AND parkedCars[]
- Stolen flag
- +1 wanted star for stealing parked car
- Different message: '🔑 Cướp xe' vs '🚗 Lên xe'

### 4. Infinite procedural city
- Chunk-based: 8x8 tiles per chunk
- Mulberry32 PRNG per chunk (deterministic)
- Chunk cache with LRU eviction (max 256)
- Roads on every 2nd chunk edge (16-tile grid)
- Buildings 85% dense, 15% grass
- Water ponds on rare chunks
- Ambient parked car spawn (3/1.5s) + despawn (1500px+)
- getTileAt(px,py) replaces tileAt(world[][])
- Minimap: 24-tile radius around player
